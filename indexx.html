About Us

GOVERNMENT MARATHI HIGHER PRIMARY SCHOOL NO 34.

This School is offering education to all children without charge.School also has a Large number of students than private students; the number of students in a class always high due to lack of facilities. The school always hire highly qualified teachers and proficient in their subject to work in a school.

Established in 1980,and is located in Angol Main Road, Angol,Belagavi. This school is approachable by all weather and is surrounded by good environment.

There are varieties of students, who come from different backgrounds with different intelligence.So, Many events like art compition ,Song compitition,dance compitions and sports meet are held to make students enthusiastic.

Co-educational and consists of grades from 1 to 7.

Uniform and shoes

Footwear helps to protect their feet from injury and from the heat and the cold. When toddlers are learning to walk, they should be in bare feet or a soft soled shoe as much as possible so they can feel what they touch with their feet and develop muscle strength.
